Lasagna

DESCRIPTION :

It consists of hollowed or halved peppers filled with any of a variety of fillings, often including meat, vegetables, cheese, rice, or sauce. The dish is usually assembled by filling the cavities of the peppers and then cooking.

Ingredients

      1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
      1 pound lean ground beef
      salt and pepper to taste
      1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
      1 clove garlic, minced
      1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
      1/2 pound shredded Cheddar cheese
